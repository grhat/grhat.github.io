EDINBURGH
Mom and Dad-

If I thought London gave off Harry Potter vibes, then Edinburgh is Hogwarts itself! Words cannot describe how magical this city is. The Gothic/medieval architecture makes me feel as though I have traveled back in time. It was foggy to the point of treacherous while hiking up Arthur's Seat, but the views at the top were spectacular. We even saw some highland cows!

Wish you were here,
Grace
